{% trans "MANAGE AND VALUE YOUR TRAILS AND TOURIST ACTIVITIES" %}

{% blocktrans %} Hikster is a webmapping software suite whose goal is to connect park managers and visitors in order to propel outdoor activities. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Park managers have advanced tools allowing - for the first time - to enhance their offer and to better understand the behavior of visitors. They can create new personalized experiences, improve park management and increase their revenues. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Visitors have interactive maps, easy to use, in order to take full advantage of the park's offer. These Maps are accessible via the web and via mobile application. {% endblocktrans %}
